No. DartsHQ works just as well with manual entry — you score using the on-screen keypad. The AI coaching, bootcamp generator, psychological profiling, Audio Coach, challenge vault, and Calibration Combine all work without any hardware. Autodarts integration unlocks real-time coordinate data for grouping drift coaching and heatmaps, but it's a bonus, not a requirement. Around half our alpha testers are manual entry players.

Audio Coach gives you a real voice in your ear between darts — corrections, encouragement, checkout routes — without you needing to look at a screen. BYOK means "bring your own key": you connect your own ElevenLabs account, which keeps the voice quality high and the cost minimal. ElevenLabs Starter is $5/month and covers heavy usage. It's available on both Free and Pro tiers. Setup takes about two minutes with a step-by-step guide inside the app.

We're in closed alpha — billing isn't live yet. All approved alpha testers get full Pro access free during the testing period. Billing launches alongside the full public release later this year. You'll get advance notice before anything changes, and the 14-day free trial applies to all new sign-ups at launch.

You can complete all 22 challenges on the Free tier and the XP is tracked in the background. When you upgrade to Pro, that banked XP is credited to your account immediately — so nothing you've earned is lost. Coaching XP from sessions applies on both tiers without restriction.
